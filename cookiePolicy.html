Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how AU Game Development Courses uses cookies on its website to improve user experience and ensure proper functionality.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences to enhance your browsing experience and optimize game development resources.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly, such as remembering your course progress or settings.

Performance Cookies: Help us analyze how users interact with our website, including tracking which game development lessons are most popular.

Functionality Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences to create a more personalized experience, like remembering your course selection.

Marketing Cookies: Deliver relevant advertising related to game development tools and track the effectiveness of our promotional campaigns.

3. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some parts of the website may not function correctly if cookies are disabled, such as not being able to track your game development course progress.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics or social media pixels to collect statistical and marketing data to improve our game development courses and related services.

5. Cookie Duration

Some cookies expire when you close your browser (session cookies), while others remain on your device until manually deleted or expired (persistent cookies). These cookies may track your interaction with game development resources on our site.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data. Please contact us if you have any concerns regarding your data or cookie usage in relation to our game development courses.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Please check back periodically for the latest version.
